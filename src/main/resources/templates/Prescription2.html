Description: Metacam is a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used for the treatment of pain and inflammation in dogs and cats. It is commonly prescribed for conditions such as osteoarthritis or post-surgical pain.

Uses: Metacam is used to reduce pain and inflammation associated with musculoskeletal disorders in dogs and cats, including arthritis and post-operative recovery.

Dosage Instructions:

For dogs, the recommended dose is:

  • Initial dose: 0.2 mg/kg on the first day of treatment.
  • Maintenance dose: 0.1 mg/kg once daily thereafter.

For cats, the dose should only be administered once under veterinary supervision.
