Description: Baytril is a broad-spectrum antibiotic used in dogs and cats to treat bacterial infections such as skin infections, respiratory infections, and urinary tract infections.

Uses: Baytril is used to treat bacterial infections in dogs and cats, including skin infections, urinary tract infections, and respiratory infections.

Dosage Instructions:

The typical dosage for Baytril is 5 mg/kg of body weight, administered orally once daily for up to 30 days, depending on the severity of the infection.

  • Follow your veterinarian's instructions for dosage and treatment duration.

Baytril is not recommended for use in growing animals as it may affect cartilage development.
