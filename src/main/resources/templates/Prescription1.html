Apoquel (Oclacitinib) - 5.4mg

Price: $129.99

Description: Apoquel is a fast-acting and safe treatment for the control of acute and chronic itching and inflammation in dogs with allergic or atopic dermatitis. It works by targeting specific inflammatory pathways and provides relief from itching within hours.

Uses: Apoquel is used to treat itching and inflammation in dogs caused by allergic reactions to environmental allergens, food, fleas, and other allergens. It helps manage symptoms of atopic dermatitis in dogs.

Dosage Instructions:

For dogs 6 months of age or older, the recommended dose of Apoquel is:

  • Initial dose: 0.4 to 0.6 mg/kg of body weight, administered orally, twice daily for up to 14 days.
  • Maintenance dose: After the initial 14-day period, administer 0.4 to 0.6 mg/kg once daily for maintenance therapy.

It is important to follow your veterinarian's specific dosage recommendations and ensure your dog receives the proper dose based on its weight and condition.
