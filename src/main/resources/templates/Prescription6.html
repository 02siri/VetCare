Cerenia (Maropitant) - 10mg Tablets

Price: $45.99

Description: Cerenia is an antiemetic medication used to prevent and treat nausea and vomiting in dogs and cats. It is commonly used for pets suffering from motion sickness or undergoing chemotherapy.

Uses: Cerenia is used to treat vomiting and nausea in dogs and cats caused by various conditions, including motion sickness and gastrointestinal issues.

Dosage Instructions:

The recommended dosage for Cerenia in dogs is 2 mg/kg of body weight, administered once daily for up to 5 consecutive days.

  • For motion sickness, administer Cerenia 2 hours before travel.

Always follow your veterinarian's dosage instructions.
